生活方式与卫生小贴士 | Lifestyle & Hygiene Tips

卫生管理

  • **勤换卫生用品：** 卫生巾/棉条应每4-6小时更换一次，月经杯根据说明使用。
  • **正确处理：** 用过的卫生用品应妥善包裹后丢弃在垃圾桶内，切勿冲入马桶。
  • **保持清洁：** 每日温水清洗外阴，避免使用刺激性洗液。淋浴是更好的选择。
  • **穿着舒适：** 选择透气、棉质内裤，保持私处干爽。

Hygiene Management

  • **Change products regularly:** Pads/tampons should be changed every 4-6 hours; menstrual cups as per instructions.
  • **Proper disposal:** Wrap used sanitary products properly and dispose of them in a trash bin; never flush them down the toilet.
  • **Maintain cleanliness:** Wash the external genital area daily with warm water, avoid irritating washes. Showering is a better option.
  • **Wear comfortable clothing:** Choose breathable, cotton underwear to keep the private area dry.

生理期期间，激素水平的波动可能导致情绪上的变化，如情绪波动、易怒、焦虑或感到悲伤。这是正常的生理反应。

应对建议

  • **自我关怀：** 给予自己足够的休息，做一些让自己放松和开心的事情。
  • **健康饮食：** 均衡饮食，减少咖啡因和糖的摄入。
  • **适度运动：** 轻柔的运动如散步、瑜伽有助于改善情绪。
  • **寻求支持：** 与亲友沟通，或在需要时寻求专业心理咨询。

During menstruation, fluctuating hormone levels can lead to emotional changes such as mood swings, irritability, anxiety, or sadness. These are normal physiological responses.

Coping Strategies

  • **Self-care:** Allow yourself enough rest and engage in activities that make you feel relaxed and happy.
  • **Healthy diet:** Eat a balanced diet, reduce caffeine and sugar intake.
  • **Moderate exercise:** Gentle exercises like walking or yoga can help improve mood.
  • **Seek support:** Communicate with friends and family, or seek professional psychological counseling if needed.

文化禁忌与迷思 | Cultural Taboos & Myths

在许多文化中，月经曾被视为禁忌或不洁。然而，这些都是基于误解的迷思。

常见迷思与真相

  • **迷思：** 月经是“不洁”的。
  • **真相：** 月经是健康的自然生理过程，是子宫为怀孕做准备后未受孕的正常排出。
  • **迷思：** 生理期女性不应参与某些活动（如烹饪、宗教仪式）。
  • **真相：** 这些都是文化习俗，没有科学依据。女性在生理期完全可以正常生活。

鼓励开放和诚实的讨论，打破围绕月经的污名。

In many cultures, menstruation has been viewed as taboo or unclean. However, these are myths based on misunderstanding.

Common Myths & Truths

  • **Myth:** Menstruation is "unclean."
  • **Truth:** Menstruation is a natural and healthy physiological process, a normal shedding of the uterine lining after preparing for pregnancy without conception.
  • **Myth:** Menstruating women should not participate in certain activities (e.g., cooking, religious ceremonies).
  • **Truth:** These are cultural customs with no scientific basis. Women can live normally during their period.

Encourage open and honest discussion to break the stigma surrounding menstruation.

常见月经疾病 | Common Menstrual Disorders

虽然月经是自然的生理过程，但有些女性可能会经历月经相关的疾病。了解这些疾病有助于早期识别和治疗。

常见类型

  • **多囊卵巢综合征 (PCOS)：** 表现为月经不规律、雄激素过高、卵巢多囊样改变。
  • **子宫内膜异位症：** 子宫内膜组织生长在子宫外，引起剧烈疼痛和不孕。
  • **子宫肌瘤：** 子宫壁上的非癌性生长，可能导致经血过多和盆腔疼痛。
  • **经前烦躁障碍 (PMDD)：** 比PMS更严重的情绪和身体症状，严重影响日常生活。

如果您怀疑自己有这些症状，请务必咨询医生进行诊断和治疗。

While menstruation is a natural physiological process, some women may experience menstrual-related disorders. Understanding these conditions helps with early recognition and treatment.

Common Types

  • **Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS):** Characterized by irregular periods, excess androgens, and polycystic ovaries.
  • **Endometriosis:** Uterine lining-like tissue grows outside the uterus, causing severe pain and infertility.
  • **Uterine Fibroids:** Non-cancerous growths on the uterine wall that can lead to heavy bleeding and pelvic pain.
  • **Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder (PMDD):** A more severe form of PMS with significant emotional and physical symptoms that severely impact daily life.

If you suspect you have any of these symptoms, please consult a doctor for diagnosis and treatment.

环保生理期选择 | Environmental Menstrual Options

传统的卫生巾和棉条会产生大量垃圾。选择环保产品有助于减少对环境的影响。

可持续产品

  • **月经杯：** 可重复使用的硅胶杯，可佩戴长达12小时，使用寿命可达数年。
  • **布卫生巾：** 可清洗和重复使用的布质卫生巾，舒适透气。
  • **可吸收内裤：** 具有吸水层的特殊内裤，可替代一次性产品。

Sustainable Products

  • **Menstrual Cups:** Reusable silicone cups that can be worn for up to 12 hours and last for several years.
  • **Cloth Pads:** Washable and reusable cloth pads, comfortable and breathable.
  • **Period Underwear:** Special underwear with an absorbent layer that can replace disposable products.

常见问题与建议 | FAQs & Tips

常见问题 (FAQs)

Q: 月经周期多长是正常的？

A: 通常是21到35天，平均28天。

Q: 痛经很严重怎么办？

A: 可以尝试热敷、适度运动、服用止痛药。如果非常严重，请咨询医生。

Q: 生理期期间可以运动吗？

A: 可以，适度运动有助于缓解不适，但应避免剧烈运动。

Tips (建议)

Q: How long is a normal menstrual cycle?

A: Typically 21 to 35 days, with an average of 28 days.

Q: What to do if menstrual cramps are severe?

A: Try warm compresses, moderate exercise, or pain relievers. If very severe, please consult a doctor.

Q: Can I exercise during my period?

A: Yes, moderate exercise can help alleviate discomfort, but intense exercise should be avoided.

何时咨询医生

  • 月经周期异常不规律或突然改变。
  • 经血量异常多或持续时间过长。
  • 剧烈疼痛，影响日常生活。
  • 月经期间发烧或出现其他不寻常症状。
  • 初潮年龄过早（8岁前）或过晚（16岁后）。

When to Consult a Doctor

  • Abnormally irregular or sudden changes in your menstrual cycle.
  • Unusually heavy bleeding or periods lasting too long.
  • Severe pain that affects daily life.
  • Fever or other unusual symptoms during your period.
  • Menarche (first period) occurring too early (before age 8) or too late (after age 16).
